Welcome to Miami

Known for its stunning beaches and vibrant nightlife, Miami is a cultural hub blending Latin American influences with American traditions. It is also a gateway to the Florida Keys and the Caribbean.

Key Details about Miami

  • Population: ~470,000
  • Incorporated: 1896
  • Region: South Florida
  • Classification: Urban
  • Income: Higher compared to Florida's average due to real estate, tourism, and international trade
