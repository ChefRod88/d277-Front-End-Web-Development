Welcome to Orlando

Orlando is famous for its world-renowned theme parks, including Walt Disney World and Universal Studios. It attracts millions of tourists every year and offers a vibrant mix of entertainment and culture.

Key Details about Orlando

  • Population: ~310,000
  • Incorporated: 1875
  • Region: Central Florida
  • Classification: Urban
  • Income: Higher compared to Florida's average due to tourism and entertainment industries
